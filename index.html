I am used to answering questions, responding to requests, documenting information clearly, and supporting users with different levels of technical comfort. What excites me most about this opportunity is the chance to contribute to a global brand by doing the quiet but essential work that keeps creative output flowing: clean structures, reliable naming, accurate metadata, and thoughtfully prepared files.
